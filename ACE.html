SRKR ACE - Top 5 Events

Prajwalan

Prajwalan is an immersive hackathon where ideas take flight. Join a passionate community to tackle real-world tech challenges. Leverage expert mentorship and hands-on workshops to build your solution. Showcase your skills and compete for exciting prizes and recognition. This is your chance to innovate and shape the future of technology.

Spurthi

Spurthi is the annual flagship fest celebrating technology and creativity. It is a grand fusion of technical and cultural events. Compete in coding ,tech and fun challenges. Simultaneously, showcase your talent in various cultural competitions. Join us for a unique platform to learn, compete, and network.

MLXplore

MLXplore is a highly focused machine learning extravaganza. It's centered on hands-on projects and intensive model-building contests. Gain practical skills through expert-led tutorials and workshops. Tackle real-world AI challenges using the latest techniques. This is a must-attend for anyone passionate about AI and data science.

SkillUp

SkillUp is a series of hands-on bootcamp. It is designed to upskill students in trending technologies. You'll gain practical mastery over essential tech tools and frameworks. This training helps bridge the gap between academia and industry. Join us to enhance your resume and kickstart your tech career.

Newbies

Newbies is our warm onboarding and orientation event. It is designed to officially welcome freshers to the ACE community. Get to know our vision, mission, and the senior members. Connect with fellow students and find your place within the club. This is the perfect way to start your journey with ACE on the right foot.
